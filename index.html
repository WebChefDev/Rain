So what is a sump pump anyhow?

A sump pump is part of a larger system that prevents water from entering your house. It works like this:

A: Weeping Tile

Weeping tile is essential for managing groundwater. These perforated pipes collect excess water from around your foundation, directing it away to prevent flooding.

B: Sump Pump Basin

The sump pump basin collects water from your basement, working in tandem with the sump pump to keep your home dry. Regularly checking the basin ensures optimal operation.

C: Sump Discharge

The sump discharge pipe carries water away from your home to a safe drainage area. In Edmonton, water is discharged to the sewer in the winter, and to the streets in the summer.
